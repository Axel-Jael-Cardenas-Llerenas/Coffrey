Tamariend jams

INGREDIENTS:

6 cups of tamarind in pieces.
3 cups of sugar.
Grated lemon.
Orange scratch.
Half cinnamon stick (optional).

Preparation:

Place pulp with seed in lukewarm water 24 hours before preparation. After that time, remove the seeds manually and liquefy with a minimum amount of soaking water, measure the pulps obtained.

for each cup of pulp add 3/4 parts of sugar cup.

Begin heating the mixture, stirring continuously but slowly until it boils. then check periodically. Cook until the point is reached.

Pack the product in sterile frazcos up to 0.5 cm below the mouth of the frazco. place the lid without closing.

inside a double-bottomed pot, with hot water that covers the frazcos in half, place for 10 minutes, with the lids only on, without fittings.

Cover the frazcos well and fill the pot with water that covers the frazcos completely. let boil for 30 min.

Once the product is cold, mark it with the name and date of manufacture

Check that the lid can not be opened easily "vacuum check", store in a dry and dark place.
