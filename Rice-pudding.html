Rice Pudding

Ingredints:

1 Tablespoon Ground Cinnamon
1/2 Cup Raisins
1 Condensed Milk Can LA LECHERA
1 CARNATION CLAVEL Evaporated Milk Can
1 Cup Rice soaked in hot water for 10 minutes and drained
4 1/2 Water Cups
1 Piece Cinnamon in raja


Preparation:

Heat the water with the cinnamon and when it boils, add the rice; cook for 15 minutes or until the rice is tender.

Pour the LA LECHERA Condensed Milk, the CARNATION CLAVEL Evaporated Milk and the raisins; mix and continue cooking at low heat for 20 to 25 minutes until lightly thickened.

Serve warm or cold and sprinkle with ground cinnamon. You can add apple cubes and blueberries.

Dairy products are high in calcium and vitamin D, which are essential for the conservation and formation of bones.
