◖Coffrey◗

Apple juice
Ingredients:

4 red apples with peel
Juice of 1 lemon
3 cups of water
1 pinch of cinnamon powder Sweetener to taste

Prepareation :


Remove the heart of the apples with a knife and cut them into 4.
In a juicer, blend the fruit and add the other ingredients, keeping the maximum speed for 1 minute.
Strain, pour into a bottle and bring to the refrigerator.
Serve ice cream.
