Lemon juice

Ingredients:

2 lemons, cut in quarters and without seeds.
8 cups of cold water
½ cup to 1 cup of sugar, adjust according to your taste Ice cubes to taste

Preparation:

Place the pieces of lemon in the blender with 4 cups of water and sugar. Blend the ingredients until a homogeneous mixture is obtained, it will remain a little foamy and with a medium white color. Strain or close the lemonade and mix with the rest of the water. Serve immediately accompanied by ice.
