Milkshakes and coconut

Ingredients:

1 coconut
Coconut water
1 liter of milk
150 grams of brown sugar
4 tablespoons cinnamon powder

PREPARATION:

Look for a wide and good container, then introduce the content of the coconut, make the coconut water, then make a liter of milk, add 150 grams of brown sugar and 4 tablespoons of cinnamon and after all that beat everything and put it to Freeze to enjoy your delicious coconut smoothie
