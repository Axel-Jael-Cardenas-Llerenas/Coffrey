Flan

Ingredients:

5 eggs
one 1 can (12oz) of evaporated milk
1 can (14oz) condensed milk
1 spoon of vanilla 1/2
1 cup of sugar
2 tablespoons of water

Preparation:

Preheat the oven to 350 ° F and keep the mold you will use for the bain-marie on hand.

Place the sugar for the caramel in a pan over medium heat with the 2 tablespoons of water until it melts evenly.

Cook until the candy acquires a golden color.

See it in the mold you will use. Spread the candy carefully through the mold until it covers the walls of the mold. Let it cool.

Add the eggs and liquefy only for a few seconds. Then add the evaporated milk and mix. Incorporate condensed milk and mix. Add vanilla, sugar and continue mixing until you get a homogeneous mixture.

Seal and pour the mixture into the mold that you already have previously caramelized.

Take the oven to a bain-marie for 45 minutes.

Verify that you are ready using a skewer stick or a fine knife. If they come clean, it means that you are ready.

Seal it from the oven with great care not to burn and put it on a wire rack. Allow to cool to room temperature until completely cool

Demolda and take to the refrigerator until the moment of serving.
