◖Coffrey◗

American coffe

Ingredients:

A giant glass
Leche de caja, or bag
Chocolate de polvito
A Very Happy Spoon
A place to prepare it


Preparation:


Take a glass and pour 5 spoons of liquid chocomilk or more to taste.
Then you pour 4 times (a lot or a little according to your taste) of Carnation Clavel milk and stir it with a spoon.
Then you put 2 tablespoons of sugar if you want so that the recipe goes and you stir again.
Then you pour the milk and stir it again.
And enjoy it.
