Cupcakes

Ingredients:

3 cups of wheat flour.
2 teaspoons of baking powder.
1/2 teaspoon of table salt.
3/4 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature.
1 1/2 cups of standard sugar
4 large eggs
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
1 1/4 cups of milk



Preparation:

Preheat the oven to 180 ° C (350 ° F). Grease 24 molds for muffin or lining with paper cups.

Mix the flour very well with the baking powder and the salt in a medium bowl.

In a separate bowl, beat the butter with the sugar until it is fluffy. While stirring, add the eggs one by one, mixing well after each addition. It incorporates vanilla.

Add the powders to the previous mixture, alternating with the milk, and mixing until all the lumps are undone, but without over churning.

Sold the dough inside the prepared molds, filling them up to three quarters of their capacity. Bake until they feel spongy to the touch, approximately 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and allow to cool inside the molds for 10 minutes. Then unmold and place on wire racks to cool completely.

In a bowl beat the butter and vanilla, until acremar. Add the icing sugar, 1 cup at a time, beating very well after each addition. Add the milk and continue beating until the bitumen is fluffy. Decorate the cold cupcakes with this bitumen.
