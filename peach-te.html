Te and peach

Ingredients:

2 liters of water
4 peaches
5 yellow lemons
4 tablespoons of honey
10 mint leaves
1 cup of ice
4 envelopes of green tea

Preparation:

Heat the water in a deep pot over medium heat and add the peaches without bone and cut into slices, cook for 10 minutes. Lower the temperature and add the lemon juice and the green tea bags.

Cook for 2 more minutes and turn off. Remove the tea bags and add the honey. Allow the tea to cool. Add the ice, mint and some slices of lemon. It serves.
