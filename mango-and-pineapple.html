Milkshakes and pineapple

Ingredients:

1 cup of water or almond milk, original / without sugar
1 small banana or ½ of a large one
2 clementines or 1 orange, peeled
1 medium ripe mango (2 cups) peeled
1 ¼ cups of frozen pineapple chunks
1 tablespoon of flax seeds

Preparation:

Put everything in the blender in the order indicated.
Mix until smooth.
Enjoy with this milkshake the flavor of the tropics.
