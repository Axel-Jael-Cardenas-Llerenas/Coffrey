Chocomilk-strawberry

Ingredients:

2 cups Milk
1 cup Strawberry chopped
2 tablespoons Cocoa powder
2 tablespoons Sugar
1/2 cup Ice

Preparation:

Blend milk with strawberries and cocoa. Add the sugar and ice little by little ..
Serve and decorate with sliced ​​strawberries.
