Frappe de chocolate

Ingredients:
200 ml. of milk.
2 tablespoons of instant coffee.
¼ cup of chocolate ice cream.
1 teaspoon of almond extract.
1 cup of crushed ice.
2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup.
Cinnamon to sprinkle.
Whipped cream to decorate.

Preparation:


Place the milk, coffee, ice cream, almond extract and crushed ice in the blender. Add the sugar if you wish.

Blend until the mixture is homogeneous.

Subsequently, pour a spoonful of chocolate syrup into the bottom of the cup of the frappé. To finish, serve the drink in it, sprinkle with cinnamon and garnish with whipped cream.
