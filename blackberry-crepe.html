Blackberry crepe

Ingredients:

1 tza. Of flour
3 tbsp of sugar
1 pinch of salt
1 ½ tzas. milk
2 eggs
1 tbsp of vanilla essence
2 tbsp of melted butter
Others:
1/2 tza of sugar glass to decorate
1/2 tza of fresh blackberries
1/2 tza of fresh raspberries

Preparation:


Licuate the ingredients of the crepes and refrigerate for 30 minutes
Pour into a hot pan a little mixture of crepes, spread flip and let cook.
Fill each crepe with a bit of the blackberry jam, place Philadelphia® Cream Cheese in Slices and fold in a triangle.
Serve by sprinkling some icing sugar on top, blackberries and raspberries
