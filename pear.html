Pear juice
Ingredients:

4 green pears
2 cup of water Juice of two lemons
½ cup sugar or your favorite sweetener
6 mint leaves
Ice to taste

Preparation:

Wash and peel the pears. Cook them together with the first amount of water, lemon juice and sugar. Turn off the first boil and let cool.
Licua the fruit with the cooking water and add the mint.
Serve in a jar with enough ice.
