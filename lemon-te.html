◖Coffrey◗

Te and lemon

Ingredients:

2 lemons
a liter of water

Preparation:

Place the liter of water on the fire.

Add the peel of 2 lemons.

Boil for 15 minutes.

When you extinguish the fire, add the juice of the 2 lemons.

Sweeten with honey, sucralose or stevia and go.
