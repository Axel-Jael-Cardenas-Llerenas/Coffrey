Tangerine and cucumber frappe

Ingredients:

2 1/2 Ice Cups
1 lemon
Agave honey to taste
7 tangerines (the juice)
Chile Tajín
1 cucumber
Agave honey to taste
2 1/2 cups of ice
1 lemon

Preparation:

Peel and chop the cucumber

Blend the cucumber with ice, lemon and agave honey until you obtain a mixture with frappé consistency. Refrigerate

Repeat the operation with the mandarin juice, ice, lemon and agave honey.

Serve in a glass first the cucumber frappé and then the tangerine. Garnish with Tajín chili.
