Philadelphia crepe

Ingredients:

1 cup of Wheat flour (140 grams)
1 Egg unit
1 tablespoon of Sugar
1 cup of Milk (240 milliliters)
2 tablespoons of melted butter

1 cup of cream cheese
½ cup of Sugar glass (70 grams)
½ lemon unit (juice and zest)
20 units of Strawberry



Preparation:

These sweet crepes with fruit are very easy to make. To start with, we prepare the crepes precisely, mixing all the ingredients to obtain a homogeneous mass.

Next, heat a pan with a little butter or oil and add a dollop of batter. Slowly lower the flame and let the dough spread throughout the pan, when bubbles start to bubble, turn it over and cook on the other side. Repeat the operation until the mass of the sweet crepes is finished.

Besides, to make the cream, mix the cheese with the electric mixer with the sugar, the juice and the lemon zest. Mix everything until you get a soft cream, you can add vanilla or any other essence if you wish.

With everything ready, we can only put together the crepes of strawberries and cream cheese. To do this, place a generous layer of the cheese mixture on each crepe and top with added slices of strawberry to taste. Then roll them up or fold them the way you like.

The crepes of strawberry and cream cheese can be served as breakfast or dessert. A very elegant option would be to cut the rolls into taquitos and to bathe with a chocolate sauce or condensed milk for the dessert table or Christmas snacks.
