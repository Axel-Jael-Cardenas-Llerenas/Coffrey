English cream jelly

Ingredientes:

2 sachets of unflavored gelatin (grenetina)
1/2 cup of water
1 can (12 oz) of evaporated milk
1 3/4 cup of goat's milk candy
2 tablespoons of brandy or vanilla, if desired Crème Anglaise
2 buds
1/4 cup of sugar
1 cup of whipping cream
1 1/2 teaspoons of brandy or vanilla
Mint leaves, to decorate

Preparation:

Spray spray in 6 oz. In a microwave container add the gelatin in the water and rest 2 minutes. Microwave 30 to 45 seconds or until dissolved.

In a quarter pot, heat the milk over medium high heat. Do not let it boil. Add the cajeta until it is mixed. Add the gelatin and the brandy. Add in the molds and refrigerate 4 to 8 hours.

In a dish mix the yolks and sugar. In a small pot over medium heat mix the cream until it boils. Add the yolks. Mix and heat for 10 minutes or thicken. Let cool and add the brandy. Let cool.

Serve unmolding with the crème anglaise. Garnish with mint leaves.
