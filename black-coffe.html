◖Coffrey◗

Black coffe

Ingredients:

1 envelope of soluble coffee
2 slices of cinnamon
1/2 cup of sugar
1 liter of water

Preparation:


Boil the water in a pot of cured clay.
Once the water is boiling, add the soluble coffee and the cinnamon sticks, let it boil for a few minutes.
Remove from the fire, add the sugar and be ready to serve.
