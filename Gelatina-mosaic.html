Jelly-Mosaic

Ingredients:

2 flavored gelatin envelopes for water
1 condensed milk
1 evaporated milk
1 half cream
2 tablespoons vanilla
4 grenetina envelopes
1/2 cup cold water

Preparation:

Make your flavored gelatins following the directions on the envelope. Once already short list in squares.

Oil your mold with cooking oil and mind to the refrigerator while you prepare your gelatin

In the half cup of water dissolve the 4 envelopes of grenetina and let stand for 8 minutes. Then bring the grenetina to the microwave for 45 seconds. (You can do it in a water bath too, until it melts completely)

in the blender the condensed milk is liquefied the evaporated the half cream and the 2 tablespoons of vanilla While still liquefying, add the melted gelatin.
Place the strawberry jelly squares in your mold and add the 3 milk mixture. Leave to set for 2 hours or more until it settles completely.
