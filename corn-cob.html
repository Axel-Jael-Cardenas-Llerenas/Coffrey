◖Coffrey◗

Corn cob

Ingredients:

6 large tender ears
6 eggs
1 bar and a half of butter of 90gr
1 can of condensed milk
1 tablespoon baking powder
3/4 cup of flour
1/2 cup whole milk
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon



Preparation:

Beat the butter with the whole eggs, one by one while continuing to beat. We slice the corn and liquefy them with the can of condensed milk and the whole milk, to incorporate to the previous preparation without stopping to beat. Add flour and baking powder. Bake in preheated oven at 150ºC between 40 and 60 minutes (go checking with a toothpick putting it in the cake and when it comes out dry it is ready, check that it does not overcool at the top) take out, cool and sprinkle sugar and canel
