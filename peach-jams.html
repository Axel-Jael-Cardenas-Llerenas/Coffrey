Peach jams

Ingredients:

1 1/2 kilos of peaches
1 kilo of sugar
1 cup of water
1 slice of cinnamon

Preparation:

Peel the peaches, remove the stones, cut them into quarters and place them in a layered container, alternating with the sugar.

Then, let them marinate for 1 hour and cook on high heat for 10 minutes. Add water and cinnamon.

Then continue cooking over a low heat, stirring with a wooden spoon when it starts to thicken.

When it is thick, leave it on the fire for 10 more minutes and remove from heat. Let cool and serve.
