Chocomilk-vainilla

Ingredients:

3 moldesitos of ice already scraped (with the scraper)
1 \ 4 cup of milk carnation (evaporated milk)
3 teaspoons of milk powder
1 \ 4 cup of water
1 teaspoon of vanilla essence
3 1 \ 2 teaspoons of sugar
1 \ 4 teaspoon artificial color powder (yellow)


Preparation:

All these ingredients are going to put into the aluminum cup that comes with the chocomilera. and you are going to liquefy them
for about 3-4 minutes, or if you have any doubts about the weather, go reviewing your chocomilk and if you look at them still in sight, give him another minute more, and you can add a little extra water in case it gets too thick.
ready, try it for flavor, and see that you need more sugar, so add a little more.
