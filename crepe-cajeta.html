Cajeta crepe

Ingredients:

1 1/2 cup sifted wheat flour
2 whole eggs
5 tbsp melted butter
enough milk to make the atolito
1 pinch salt
1 cup of cajeta or dulce de leche according to what is said in each country
1/4 liquid cream
chopped nut

Preparation:

In a bowl mix everything (except the cajeta and the cream), the milk is poured little by little until the point of a light atole is beaten strong with a balloon so that there are no lumps, it is left to rest around 15 minutes

Put a Teflon pan to make it very hot with a little broach or napkin, put a little oil or melted butter, put a little of the atole and move the pan in circles that extend to the size of the pan and when you can see that it is cooked so that it is cooked on the other side.

The cajeta is put on fire with the cream and it is left to heat up until this aguadita, individual plates are put from two to three crepes folded in a triangle and on top the hot cajeta is poured to the taste and a bit of chopped walnut is put on top
