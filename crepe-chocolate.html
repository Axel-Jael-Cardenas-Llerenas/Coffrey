Chocolate crepe

Ingredients:

1/2 cup cow's milk
2 butter spoons
1/2 teaspoon of vanilla essence
3 pieces of egg
2 tablespoons of white sugar
1 cup of wheat flour
1/4 cup cocoa powder

Preparation:

Blend the ingredients until a smooth mixture is obtained. If there are lumps, you can strain the mixture.

In a small pan (the size you want the crepe) non-stick and hot (or with a little butter), put a little dough and move the pan to cover the entire bottom with the mixture. Let cook until the crepe begins to peel off the edges, and is cooked on the bottom.

Turn it over to finish cooking. Remove it from the pan, and reserve.
