Strawberry crepe

Ingredients:

2 packs of 150 gr of Cream Cheese Philadelphia 4 cditas. of sugar 2 pinches of ground cinnamon

1 tza. of wheat flour 1 ½ tza. milk 2 pcs egg 3 tbsp of sugar 1 pinch of salt 2 tbsp of melted butter

2 cups. of strawberries ½ tza. of sugar ¼ tza. of water



Preparation:

Place strawberries, water and sugar in a pan and bring to a boil until strawberries are softened.

Licuate crepe ingredients and refrigerate for 30 minutes Pour in a hot pan a little mixture of crepes, spread, turn and let cook

. Mix Philadelphia Cream Cheese with sugar and cinnamon.

Untar crepes with Philadelphia Cream Cheese and place cooked strawberries; fold in triangle shape and decorate to taste.
