Strawberry jams

Ingredients:

1 kilo of strawberries, clean
4 cups of white sugar
1/4 cup of real lemon juice

Preparation:

In a large bowl, mash the strawberries little by little until you have 4 cups of crushed fruit. In a cast iron casserole, mix strawberries, sugar and lemon juice. Cook over low heat, continuing to move, until the sugar has dissolved. Raise the heat to high and let the mixture boil, continuing to move, until it reaches a temperature of 105 ° C (220 ° F).


Go into sterilized and hot bottles, leaving about 1 cm of space between the jam and the bottle neck. Close well. Cool in a water bath if you are going to consume immediately, otherwise, just refrigerate.
