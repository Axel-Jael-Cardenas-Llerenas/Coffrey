Jelly-Oreo-cookie

Ingredients:

210 g of Cream Cheese Philadelphia
1 large package of Oreo® cookie in pieces.
1 L of milk
3/4 tza sugar
3/4 tza. cold water
28 g of grenetine

Preparation:

Hydratation Glutenin with water.

Licuate 1/2 L of milk with Philadelphia® Cream Cheese and sugar.

Heat 1/2 L of remaining milk and add the hydrated gelatin, mix until completely dissolved.

Remove from the fire and mix with the previously liquefied.

Pour into a jelly pan, add Oreo cookie and refrigerate until curd.

Remove and decorate to taste.
