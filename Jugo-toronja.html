Juice-grapefruit

Ingredients:

6 cups of water 2 pieces of grapefruit 1/4 cup of agave honey ice to taste

Preparation:

Cut the grapefruit, remove the seeds and squeeze the juice. Add to the jar with the water, add the honey and stir well. Serve in tall glasses with ice
