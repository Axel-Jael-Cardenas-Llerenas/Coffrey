Shell

Ingredients:

2 1/2 teaspoons (10 grams) of dry yeast
1/2 cup (120 milliliters) of warm water
1/2 cup (120 milliliters) of evaporated milk
3/8 cup (75 grams) of refined sugar
1 teaspoon salt

1/3 cup (75 grams) of melted butter
1 egg
4 cups (500 grams) of wheat flour
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon



Preparation:

Dissolve the yeast in the water in a large bowl. Add the evaporated milk, 3/8 cup of sugar, 1/3 cup of butter, salt, egg and 2 cups of flour; mix with a spatula. Gradually add the 2 cups of remaining flour and 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon. Place on a floured surface and knead only until a dough forms.

Knead for 6 to 8 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic. Place inside a bowl greased with oil and move the dough inside it so that it is covered with oil. Cover with a towel and let it rest in a warm place so that it doubles its size, approximately 1 hour.

Meanwhile, prepare the shell cover. Beat in a bowl 2/3 cup of sugar with 1/2 cup of butter, until acremar. Add the flour and continue beating until it forms a thick paste. Divide the pasta into 2 portions and place each one in a separate bowl. Mix the cinnamon with one portion and the vanilla with the other.

When the dough has doubled its volume, cut it into 12 pieces of the same size. Form balls and arrange them in a greased tray, 8 centimeters apart.

Now take the cinnamon flavor paste and divide it into 6 balls. Flatten each ball with your hands and place on a ball of dough, pressing gently to flatten the dough a little. Repeat the procedure with the vanilla flavor paste and the rest of the dough balls. With the help of a knife, mark lines similar to those of seashells. Cover and let them double their volume for approximately 45 minutes.
