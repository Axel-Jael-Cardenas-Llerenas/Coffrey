Nutella crepe

Ingredients:

1 1/2 cups of milk
1 cup of flour
2 eggs
1 tablespoon oil
1/4 teaspoon of salt
2 teaspoons of sugar
2 ripe bananas in slices
1/2 bottle of nutella or chocolate



Preparation:

In a blender, put milk, flour, eggs, oil, salt and sugar. Stir well. Light the stove over medium heat and put a frying pan.

Put a few drops of oil on a small and flat pan and make sure to cover the whole pan to avoid sticking the crepes. (It should not be full of grease, so you can clean it with a napkin to absorb some of the oil).

With a soup spoon, put some of the blender mixture in the pan and cover the entire surface of the pan with a layer of the mixture. Wait for it to begin to brown and turn it around quickly.

When the crepe is ready (it should be in less than 1 minute) put on a plate and reserve. Continue the same steps with the rest of the mix.

Once all the crepes are ready, fill each one with a few spoonfuls of melted chocolate and a few slices of bananas.
