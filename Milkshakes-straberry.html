◖Coffrey◗

Milkshakes and straberry.

Ingredients:

1/2 glass of milk
5 spawns
4 scoops of vanilla ice cream
Chocolate chips

Preparation:

Put the milk in a blender glass and add the spawns.
Beat with the blender and pour in two glasses.
To decorate the spawn shake, add sparks and serve.
