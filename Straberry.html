Milkshakes mango and straberry

Ingredients:

1 handle
1 cup of frozen strawberries
1 frozen banana (you can use a regular banana too, only the shake will be less thick)
¼ cup of yogurt
2-4 tablespoons of fruit juice (can use pineapple)

Preparation:

Combine the mango, strawberries, banana, yogurt and 2 tablespoons of fruit juice in the blender until smooth. Add the additional fruit juice until reaching the desired consistency. Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator for up to 2 days
